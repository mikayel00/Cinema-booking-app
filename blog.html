THE MOST DISCUSSED

Black handsome Samuel leads a fun life as a yacht captain, arranging fun swims for money.
True, the yacht belongs to Samantha, but Samuel's charm allows the hero to break the rules and
often throw parties. One day, a girl with a child appears on the pier, and after a short
conversation it turns out that Gloria is Samuel's three-month-old daughter. Kristin deceives
the guy and, leaving Gloria, flies to London. Not wanting to become a single father, and indeed
the father of a child, Samuel goes to London, wanting to find Christina and return the girl.
However, it turns out that the information about Christina's work is false. Upon learning that
her captain left the country without permission, Samantha dismisses Samuel and he remains in
London without money and with a child in his arms. The hero is unexpectedly lucky when he sees how
Samuel arranges a performance at the subway station so as not to lose Gloria, producer Bernie
offers the former captain a job as a stuntman. The contract turns out to be so profitable that in
the following years Gloria's life turns into a solid holiday of childhood. However, ahead of Gloria
and Samuel are waiting for life's trials and the unexpected appearance of the missing Christina.
Whether the heroes will be able to overcome the trials, see the drama film "2 + 1".
